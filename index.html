About

I build L&D programs that perform — and last.

With a background spanning instructional design, adult learning theory, and complex program operations, I sit at a rare intersection: the ability to design rigorous learning experiences and build the systems that make them run.

I've led global L&D initiatives, built content operations infrastructure from scratch, and designed programs for high-stakes audiences — including healthcare professionals and credentialing organizations — where clarity and quality aren't optional. My work in medical credentialing has sharpened skills that transfer anywhere: precision, stakeholder management, and building for scale.

Good L&D design is invisible. When a program works, learners don't notice the architecture — they just move forward with confidence.
